About Diana

Diana Krykalova is a talented artist specializing in portraits and landscapes. Originally from Ukraine, Diana now resides in Germany, bringing a unique blend of Eastern European and Western influences to her art. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for capturing the essence of her subjects, Diana's work has garnered acclaim from art enthusiasts around the world.
